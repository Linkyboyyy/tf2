Game Overview

Team Fortress 2 is a team-based multiplayer shooter where players choose from 9 unique classes, each with its own strengths and roles. The game emphasizes strategy, coordination, and good old-fashioned chaos.

All Classes

Scout

Fast and agile. Captures points twice as fast.

Soldier

Slow but powerful. Uses a rocket launcher and rocket jumps.

Pyro

Wields a flamethrower. Loves fire. Unclear if insane.

Demoman

Uses sticky bombs and grenades to control areas.

Heavy

Big man with a minigun. Lots of health and firepower.

Engineer

Builds turrets, dispensers, and teleporters for the team.

Medic

Heals teammates. Can unleash an ÜberCharge for invincibility.

Sniper

Takes out enemies from afar with deadly precision.

Spy

Disguises as enemies, goes invisible, and backstabs for instant kills.
